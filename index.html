What is SpaceBook?

Spacebook matches Event-space owners with Event-Organizers who request spaces through our websit, and customers pay automatically through Website.
Whether you’re trying to offset costs of your car, cover this month’s bills, or fund your dreams, Lyft will get you there. So, go ahead. Be your own boss!!

Why SpaceBook?

For Space Owners

Space owners signup with some basic information about the space,location,and their contact info. After approval by the SpaceBook team,they add more information about the space,upload pictures, and select the types of events they welcome in their space.
When an event organizer contacts a space owner, they will reply with further information about availability and prices. The space owner is responsible for making sure the space is use-ready before every event.
SpaceBook will handle back-end details to guarantee a safe and secure exchange all round. SpaceBook will also facilitate reviews following the event to make sure wonderful spaces and hosts get proper notice.

For Event Organizers

Spacebook can browse listings and filter by size, location, type of activity, or keyword. Once users find a space they like, they can connect with the host to receive additional information. They can search Spacebook's Friend's for event production services and equipment, if they wish.
Finally, when they find a perfect match, they book the space online and have an unforgettable event!
